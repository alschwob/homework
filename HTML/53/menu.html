Steak with Potatoes and Asparagus Plate

A succulent grilled steak cooked to perfection, served with a side of buttery mashed potatoes and lightly seasoned roasted asparagus. The steak is tender, juicy, and full of flavor, complemented by the creamy potatoes and the crisp, fresh asparagus.

Delmonica Steak and Fries Plate

A classic Delmonica steak, also known as a ribeye steak, grilled to your preferred level of doneness. It is accompanied by a generous portion of golden and crispy french fries. The steak is well-marbled, resulting in a rich and flavorful taste, while the fries provide the perfect satisfying crunch.

Salmon Plate

A delightful dish featuring a perfectly seared salmon fillet served on a bed of flavorful grains or steamed vegetables. The salmon is tender, flaky, and infused with a delicate blend of herbs and spices. It is a healthy and delicious option that is sure to please seafood lovers.

Garden Salad

A refreshing and vibrant salad bursting with seasonal vegetables, crisp lettuce, and a variety of toppings. The garden salad is a colorful medley of flavors and textures, topped with a light dressing that enhances the freshness of the ingredients. It is a perfect choice for those seeking a lighter option.

Greens Salad

A nutritious and invigorating salad made with an assortment of fresh greens, such as spinach, arugula, and kale. The greens salad is accompanied by an array of toppings, such as cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and your choice of dressing. It is a delightful way to enjoy a healthy and satisfying meal.

Bar Menu

Classic Martini

A timeless cocktail made with gin or vodka, vermouth, and garnished with an olive or a twist of lemon. The classic martini is a sophisticated and iconic drink, known for its smoothness and elegance.

Old Fashioned

A traditional whiskey-based cocktail made with muddled sugar, bitters, and a splash of water. It is garnished with an orange peel and a maraschino cherry. The old fashioned is a classic choice for those who appreciate the rich flavors of bourbon or rye whiskey.

Mojito

A refreshing and tropical cocktail made with rum, muddled mint leaves, lime juice, sugar, and soda water. The mojito is a light and invigorating drink that perfectly balances sweetness and citrus flavors, making it a popular choice during warmer seasons.

Margarita

A beloved tequila-based cocktail made with freshly squeezed lime juice, triple sec, and a touch of sweetness. It is typically served in a salt-rimmed glass and garnished with a lime wedge. The margarita is a tangy and festive drink that is synonymous with celebration and relaxation.

Whiskey Sour

A classic whiskey cocktail made with whiskey, lemon juice, sugar, and sometimes an egg white for added frothiness. The whiskey sour strikes a perfect balance between tartness and sweetness, making it a timeless choice for whiskey enthusiasts.
